Important Summary

Session Fee: ₹70 per 1-hour session (fixed fee)

Free Cancellation: Up to 24 hours before your scheduled session

1. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your booked mentorship session at any time. To cancel a session, please contact us via email at sshelp37@gmail.com.

2. Refund Policy

  • Full Refund: If you cancel your session at least 24 hours before the scheduled start time, you are eligible for a full refund of ₹70.
  • No Refund: If you cancel your session less than 24 hours before the scheduled start time, no refund will be provided.
  • Mentor-Initiated Cancellation: If we need to cancel your session due to unforeseen circumstances, you will receive a full refund or the option to reschedule at no additional cost.

3. Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days and credited back to the original payment method used during booking.
